Top three activities to do in Saint Petersburg

Attend a ballet, opera, or orchestral concert

The Mariinsky Theatre is one of the most famous theatres in the world and is known for its opulent architecture and exceptional performances. The theatre has hosted some of the greatest performers in history, including Tchaikovsky and Rachmaninoff, and continues to attract top talent today.

Explore the Hermitage Museum

The State Hermitage Museum is one of the largest and oldest museums in the world, housing an incredible collection of art and cultural artifacts from around the globe, which include works by Da Vinci, Rembrandt, and Picasso, as well as ancient Egyptian and Greek artifacts.
